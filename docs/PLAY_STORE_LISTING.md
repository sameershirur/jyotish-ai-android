Jyotish AI brings authentic Vedic astrology (Jyotish) to your pocket, powered by precise
astronomical calculations and AI interpretation.

Generate your birth chart (Kundli) instantly — fully offline, no internet required:
- Lagna (ascendant), all 9 Grahas (planets), and 12 Houses
- Yoga and Dosha detection
- Vimshottari Dasha timeline (mahadasha + antardasha)
- Traditional South Indian chart display

Sign in (free) to unlock:
- AI-generated chart interpretation in plain language
- Cloud sync — access your saved charts from the web app too
- Detailed readings with adjustable depth (Beginner / Intermediate / Expert) on Pro

Free plan: 1 saved chart, summary AI reading.
Pro plan: unlimited saved charts, detailed readings, divisional charts, transit analysis,
compatibility matching, and muhurta (auspicious timing) — same subscription as the web app.
